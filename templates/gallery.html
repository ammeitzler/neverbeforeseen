Terms and Conditions

Never Before Seen In Person is a series of paintings created and seen only by the artist.

Each painting can be purchased as an an NFT. Upon the purchase of an artwork, the new owner will be transferred the NFT and the artwork will remain in its sealed canister inside of the artist’s storage facility for the entirety of its life. When the sealed canister is exhibited publicly, the purchaser’s name will be listed alongside the work.
